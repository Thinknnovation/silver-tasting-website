# Silver Tasting — Brand Guide

## Brand Overview

Silver Tasting is a premium whiskey tasting experience located in La Mesa, Cundinamarca, Colombia.

The brand focuses on delivering elegant, intimate, and cinematic whiskey tasting events for people who appreciate luxury, culture, and sensory experiences.

---

# Brand Personality

Silver Tasting should feel:

- Elegant
- Sophisticated
- Warm
- Exclusive
- Cinematic
- Intimate
- Cultural
- Premium

The experience should resemble a luxury private whiskey club rather than a conventional event website.

---

# UI Principles

## Minimalism

Avoid visual clutter.

Use:
- generous spacing
- large typography
- elegant contrast
- focused sections

---

## Premium Feel

The website should never look generic.

Avoid:
- standard bootstrap appearance
- oversaturated colors
- cheap gradients
- overly playful UI

---

## Emotional Experience

The user should feel:

> "I am entering an exclusive experience."

not:

> "I am browsing a simple events website."

---
